1. Grilled Cheese Sandwich

A warm and crispy sandwich with melted cheese inside.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1â€“2 slices of cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place one slice (butter side down) on a pan.
  3. Add cheese on top of the bread.
  4. Cover with the second slice of bread (butter side up).
  5. Cook until golden brown on both sides and cheese is melted.

2. Scrambled Eggs

Soft and fluffy eggs cooked quickly in a pan.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 teaspoon butter or oil

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and mix with a fork.
  2. Heat butter in a pan.
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan.
  4. Stir gently until cooked.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste.

3. Pasta with Tomato Sauce

Simple pasta mixed with warm and tasty tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt
  • (Optional) garlic or cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot and add salt.
  2. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  4. Add tomato sauce and warm it up.
  5. Mix the pasta with the sauce and serve.
